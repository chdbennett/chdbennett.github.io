The Future of Quantum Computing

To make quantum frictionless for everyone from developers to researchers and enthusiasts, we’re innovating across the entire stack.

We're pioneering specialized hardware and building libraries of circuits to empower researchers, developers and businesses to tap into quantum as a service through the cloud, using their preferred coding language and without having to be quantum experts themselves.

Together, we will maximize the potential of quantum through the combination of powerful technologies and open-source innovation.

Strategic workstreams

Quantum Information Science

We're unpacking and improving the fundamental building blocks of quantum, from entanglement and superposition to the development of quantum algorithms.

Quantum hardware

We’re building state-of-the-art hardware to push the boundaries of Quantum Volume even as we expand the number of qubits in our quantum systems.

Circuits and software

We're exploring and testing potential applications of quantum by building an expansive library of quantum circuits and creating tools that allow you to develop your own quantum software.

Quantum community

We’re putting our tools in the hands of developers, researchers, and business professionals around the world to harness the power of open-source innovation.
