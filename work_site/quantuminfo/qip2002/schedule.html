Preliminary Schedule

Overview

Each morning buses will leave the Hampton Inn hotel in Elmsford and go to the  IBM Yorktown Lab where the conference sessions are held.  Each evening buses will return to the hotel.  On Tuesday evening, for those attending the play "Proof" in New York City, we will organize transportation to the Tarrytown rail station in time to get to the play.  On Wednesday evening, buses will return to the hotel after the banquet dinner at the lab. On Thursday a final bus will leave the lab at 14:00 in time to catch the 14:39 train at Tarrytown, arriving in Grand Central at 15:19.
